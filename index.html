MHACbase

MHACbase is a compact microbiome database for the hypoxic zone of the Yangtze River Estuary in China, currently encompassing both metagenomic and metaproteomic data. We obtained 4,670,825 non-redundant genes and 1,559 species-level genomes above medium quality from 1.31 Tbp metagenome data.
